Brand Guidelines

Logo Rules

Our logo system is designed to be flexible yet consistent. These rules ensure the brand remains recognizable and premium across all applications.

Primary Logo

The primary wordmark is the core of our visual identity. It should be used in most applications where space allows.

  • Clear Space: Minimum 60px on all sides (desktop), 30px (mobile).
  • Minimum Size: 120px width for screen, 25mm for print.
  • Usage: Do not crowd the logo. It needs room to breathe to maintain its elegance.

Stacked Logo

This alternate layout offers a more compact structure while maintaining elegance and legibility.

  • Usage: Use when horizontal space is limited (e.g., vertical banners, centre-aligned packaging).
  • Alignment: Always keep centred. Do not left-align the stacked version.

Initial Mark “E”

The monogram is a simplified brand asset used when the full wordmark would be too detailed or overpowering.

  • Usage: Social media avatars, favicon, envelope seals, watermark.
  • Restriction: Never replace the primary logo with the initial mark on the main website header.
