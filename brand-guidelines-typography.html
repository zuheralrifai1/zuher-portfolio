Brand Guidelines

Typography

Type is the voice of the brand. Our selection balances classic elegance with modern clarity.

Primary Typeface

Didot

A high-contrast serif that feels intellectual, fashionable, and heritage-driven. Used for headlines and large display text.

  • Usage: Headlines, Titles, Quotes.
  • Style: Use sparingly to maintain impact.
Secondary Typeface

Sans Source

Clean, geometric, and modern. It balances the traditional feel of Didot.

  • Usage: Subtitles, captions, small details.
  • Character: Neutral but confident.
Body Copy

Calibri

Note: The prompt specified Calibri for body text. In web context, this is often a system font fallback or a deliberate understated choice for accessibility and familiarity.

  • Usage: Long-form text, letters, invoices.
  • Benefit: Highly legible and unassuming.
