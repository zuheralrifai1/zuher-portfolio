Print Collateral

How the brand identity translates into physical, printed touchpoints.

Print materials were designed to enhance the brand’s premium positioning and provide a clear, consistent experience across offline interactions. The focus was on quality, readability, and tactile detail.

Business Cards

The Elegant House business cards were designed as a compact expression of the brand – tactile, restrained, and clearly structured.

1. Embossed Logo for Premium Tactility

The front of the card features an embossed wordmark, allowing the brand to be felt as well as seen. This raised effect reinforces the workshop’s craftsmanship and attention to detail.

2. Deep Hague Blue Background

Using the brand’s signature Hague Blue creates a strong visual presence and instantly signals a high-end positioning. The dark, muted tone also enhances the embossed effect and keeps the design timeless.

3. Calm, Balanced Typography

The serif logotype paired with a simple sans-serif for details creates a sophisticated contrast. Generous spacing gives the layout room to breathe and elevates the sense of luxury.

4. Clear, Functional Information Layout

The reverse side features a crisp vertical divider separating the QR code from the contact details. This creates an organised structure and guides the eye naturally across the card.

5. QR Code for Modern Accessibility

Including a QR code adds a contemporary touch and lets customers navigate directly to the website or booking form – merging traditional craftsmanship with modern usability.

Leaflet Design

How the Elegant House identity is expressed in printed marketing materials.

Leaflet Design

The Elegant House leaflet was created as a high-impact physical marketing piece aimed at local customers. It introduces the brand, showcases its craftsmanship, and provides a clear way for customers to get in touch.

Photography-Led Layout

The leaflet relies on strong, character-rich photography of real furniture pieces – not stock imagery. Full-cover images across the panels help create an immersive impression of quality and craft.

Clear Front-Cover Hierarchy

The front cover combines a bold hero image with the wordmark and supporting tagline, establishing heritage and credibility before the leaflet is even opened.

Elegant Colour Blocking

Inside panels use Drop Cloth, Hague Blue, and Light Blue tones to separate content while staying faithful to the brand palette. These neutral tones also improve readability and create calm visual structure.

Organised, Reader-Friendly Structure

Each panel is designed with purpose:
– Services overview
– Brand statement or quote
– Contact information and QR code

Inside, content is structured into mission, reasons to choose Elegant House, and testimonials — giving readers a complete story at a glance.

Balanced Typography & QR Integration

Serif headings bring elegance, while sans-serif body copy keeps information clean and approachable. The QR code links directly to digital channels, blending traditional print with modern utility.

Labels & Tags

Labels and tags extend The Elegant House identity onto individual pieces and packaging. They are small but critical touchpoints, reinforcing quality and ownership every time a customer interacts with a product.

Design Approach

  • Minimal, craft-focused aesthetic – clean layout featuring the serif wordmark, keeping the brand calm, premium, and handcrafted.
  • Consistent use of brand palette – soft neutrals and Hague Blue ensure labels remain subtle yet recognisable.
  • High legibility at small sizes – typography is adjusted for clarity without losing elegance.

Applications

  • Fabric label – small woven tag on upholstered pieces, unobtrusive but clearly branded.
  • Sticker roll – used on packaging, protective wrapping, and workshop organisation while staying visually consistent.
  • Hang tags – attached to new or restored pieces, communicating professionalism and adding perceived value at handover.

Outcome

A subtle, premium brand presence on every physical touchpoint, from workshop to customer home.

Print collateral for The Elegant House brings the brand into the physical world with the same clarity, restraint, and crafted detail seen online. From cards to leaflets and labels, every piece is designed to feel premium, intentional, and consistent.
