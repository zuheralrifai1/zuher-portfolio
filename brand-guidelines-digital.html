Digital Presence

How 'The Elegant House' identity is applied across digital touchpoints.

Website Homepage

The homepage establishes the brand’s digital first impression — calm, elegant, and centred on craftsmanship. The layout prioritises storytelling through large imagery, refined typography, and generous spacing.

  • Clean, uncluttered composition
  • Emphasis on warm photography and lifestyle presentation
  • Clear service explanations with Didot headers
  • Consistent use of brand colours
  • Typography reflects the premium tone of the brand

Navigation & Structure

Navigation is designed to be clean, intuitive, and free of visual clutter. The structure uses clear category labels and simple dropdowns, ensuring users can move through the site effortlessly.

  • Minimal, distraction-free top bar
  • Clear section titles using Didot
  • Predictable user flow between service categories
  • Dropdown menus provide depth without overwhelming the user
